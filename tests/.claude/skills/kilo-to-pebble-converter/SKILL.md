## Overall guidance
- Convert kilo units to pebble units
- Multiply the kilo amount by 3.14 to get the pebble amount
- Provide the result clearly showing both the input kilo amount and the converted pebble amount

## Conversion formula
- pebble = kilo × 3.14

## Example
- 2 kilo = 2 × 3.14 = 6.28 pebble
